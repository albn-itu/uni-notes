# Team Environment
The environment a team works in can influence it's performance. The team needs to be:

- Dedicated
- Funded
- Autonomous

While the company should provide:

- Leadership Support
- Coaching
- Customer Access
- Resource Access
- Strategy
- Guidance in the right direction
- Goals
